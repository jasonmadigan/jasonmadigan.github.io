Features

Built for transfers
that fight back.

Unreliable servers. Dropped connections. Files that vanish mid-transfer. Snag was built for all of it.

⚡

Multi-Threaded

Splits files into parallel chunks using HTTP Range requests. Each thread grabs its piece simultaneously. More threads, more speed.

🔄

Infinite Retry

When a chunk fails, Snag retries with exponential backoff. Server 500? Timeout? It picks up from the exact byte it left off. Configurable per transfer.

📦

.snag Bundles

Partial transfers are saved as .snag packages. Quit the app, restart your Mac, come back next week. Your transfer is right where you left it.

📶

Network Aware

Monitors your connection. When you lose WiFi and reconnect, Snag automatically resumes errored transfers. No manual intervention.

🍪

Cookie Auth

Paste a cookies.txt or JSON export from your browser. Snag matches cookies to domains and authenticates your transfers seamlessly.

⚖️

No Bloat

No torrents. No crypto mining. No built-in browser. No subscription. Just a native macOS app that transfers files. That's it.
