Snag - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026

Snag is a macOS file transfer application developed by Jason Madigan.

Data Collection

Snag does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting sent to third parties
  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No data shared with third parties

Local Data

Snag stores transfer metadata (URLs, filenames, progress) locally on your Mac. This data never leaves your device. Files are saved to the location you choose.

Network

Snag makes HTTP/HTTPS requests only to the URLs you provide for file transfers. No other network connections are made.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact himself@jasonmadigan.com.
