## Privacy Policy for Snag

**Last updated: 30 March 2026**

Snag is a macOS download manager developed by Jason Madigan.

### Data Collection

Snag does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting sent to third parties
- No user accounts or sign-in
- No data shared with third parties

### Local Data

Snag stores download metadata (URLs, filenames, progress) locally on your Mac. This data never leaves your device. Download files are saved to the location you choose.

### Network

Snag makes HTTP/HTTPS requests only to the URLs you provide for downloading files. No other network connections are made.

### Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact jason@jasonmadigan.com.
